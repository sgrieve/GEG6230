# Plotting exercises

Create the following graphs:

1. A histogram of precipitation for any of the catchments
1. Create a 2x2 grid of subplots, with each subplot showing the normalised histogram of precipitation for a catchment
1. A scatter plot comparing two catchment variables
1. A violin plot of the last 30 days of discharge data for each of the four catchments
1. A bar plot of landcover percentages, where every bar is a different colour
1. A line plot of all 4 catchment's maximum temperatures for a 14 day period
1. A scatter plot of the morning temperature range vs the evening temperature range for a catchment
